☕ Coffee Shop Sales Dashboard (Excel Project)

📊 Project Overview :-

This project focuses on analyzing coffee shop sales data using Microsoft Excel.
An interactive dashboard is created to track sales performance, product trends, and customer behavior across different locations and time periods
The analysis helps in understanding key factors that drive revenue, identifying high-performing products, and evaluating store-level performance.This enables better decision-making and supports business growth through data-driven insights


🎯 Problem Statement :-

The objective of this project is to analyze coffee shop sales data to evaluate overall business performance and identify key factors driving revenue.


* Understanding total sales and customer footfall
* Identifying top-performing products and categories
* Evaluating performance across different locations and time periods
* Sales trends across weekdays
* Performance by different locations
* Category-wise contribution to total sales

📁 Dataset Information :-
* Dataset Name: Coffee Shop Sales Data
* Total Records: 149117
* Columns Included: Transaction Id,Transaction Date,Transaction Time,Transaction Quantity,Store Id,Store Location,Product Id,Unit Price,Product Category,Product Type,Product Detail,Size,Total Amount etc..

🛠️ Tools & Skills Used

* Microsoft Excel
* Data Cleaning & Transformation
* Pivot Tables
* Pivot Charts
* Slicers (Filters)
* Dashboard & KPIs Design


📌 Dashboard Features

The dashboard includes the following visualizations:

* 📅 Sales by Weekdays (Bar Chart)
* 🔢Quantity Ordered Hourly (Area Chart)
* 🍩 Sales by Category (Donut Chart)
* 📦 Product Size Distribution (Pie Chart)
* 🌍 Footfall and Sales by City (Column Chart)
* 🔝 Top 5 Products by Sales (Bar Chart)
* 🔻 Bottom 5 Products by Sales (Bar Chart)
* 🎛️ Interactive Filters (Month, Days & Location slicers)


 📈 Key Insights :-

 🔢 Key Performance Metrics (KPIs)

* *Total Sales:* ₹698,812.33
* *Total Footfall:* 149,116 customers
* *Average Sales per Customer:* ₹4.69
* *Average Order Quantity:* 1.44 items per transaction


📊 Business Insights

* The business generated *₹698K+ in total revenue, supported by a high customer footfall of **149K+*, indicating strong overall demand

* Despite high footfall, the *average spending per customer (₹4.69)* is relatively low, suggesting an opportunity to increase revenue through upselling or combo offers

* The *average order quantity of 1.44 items* indicates that most customers purchase only one item per visit, highlighting potential for cross-selling strategies

* Sales peak on *Monday and Friday*, indicating higher customer engagement at the beginning and end of the week

* The *coffee category* contributes the highest share of total revenue, making it the core business driver

* *Large-sized products* are the most preferred by customers, suggesting demand for higher-value purchases

* The *Astoria location* generates the highest sales and footfall, making it the top-performing outlet

* *Barista Espresso* is the best-selling product, contributing significantly to total revenue

* *Green beans* are among the lowest-performing products, indicating low customer demand and a need for better positioning or promotion


🧠 Conclusion :-

The analysis highlights strong customer footfall and consistent revenue generation, but also reveals opportunities to improve average customer spending and order size.

By focusing on upselling, cross-selling, and optimizing low-performing products, the business can significantly enhance its overall profitability.
